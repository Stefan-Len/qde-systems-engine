# Community Rules Preview

This document will provide a public preview of the private Discord/community
rules.

Planned rules:

- use a verified identity approved by QDE-Systems;
- do not share account balances, account sizes, broker statements, or live
  position screenshots;
- do not share API keys, IBKR credentials, Databento keys, tokens, 2FA codes, or
  other secrets;
- do not post trade instructions such as "buy here" or "sell here";
- do not ask other members to copy trades;
- do not treat shared metrics as signals;
- share only sanitized logs, audit excerpts, screenshots, metrics, and issue
  reports;
- keep discussion technical, respectful, and evidence-based.

The full rules will apply inside the private member area.
