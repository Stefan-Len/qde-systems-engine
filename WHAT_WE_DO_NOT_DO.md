# What We Do Not Do

This document will define the hard boundaries of QDE Systems Group.

QDE Systems Group does not provide:

- financial advice;
- investment advice;
- trading advice;
- trade signals;
- market calls;
- copy trading;
- managed accounts;
- pooled capital;
- broker services;
- broker recommendations;
- performance guarantees;
- funded-account challenge advice;
- private trade coaching;
- live trading desk support.

Members operate their own systems independently, with their own accounts,
configs, brokers, data feeds, risk limits, and responsibility.
