# Membership

This document will describe the high-level membership model for QDE-Systems
Group.

Planned content:

- limited operator cohorts;
- ES cohort first;
- future instrument-specific cohorts may be separate;
- access is for verified licensed members only;
- source-code license and community access are related but distinct;
- membership does not create company ownership, partnership, employment, fund
  participation, managed-account relationship, or investment club membership;
- access may be limited or removed for rule breaches.

Exact pricing, availability, delivery, and license terms will be handled through
the applicable private sales channel and written terms.
