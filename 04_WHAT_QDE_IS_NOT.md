# What QDE Is Not

It is just as important to make clear what QDE is not.

This is not a signal service. It does not provide financial, investment, or
trading advice. It does not tell you what to buy or sell, and it does not
guarantee that a given setup, model output, or decision path will produce
profit.

This is not a profit guarantee. It does not promise win rate, monthly return,
funded-account pass, drawdown-free operation, or live trading result. If someone
is looking for a quick result promise, this is not that product.

This is not a ready-made LIVE broker bot. The system may have a LIVE runtime
direction, but the commercial package does not include a built-in production
broker adapter, does not automatically connect to a broker, and does not take
over broker-side responsibility. Broker-backed PAPER or production LIVE use
requires a buyer-side broker adapter, market-data setup, credential handling,
testing, validation, risk review, audit review, and your own decision.

That does not need to be scary. The broker side is not something to connect
"live" on day one, and it is not useful to think about it that way. First, the
system operation, DEMO / LEARNING process, audit, and risk boundaries need to be
understood. The broker-connected direction is a separate step with separate
validation.

This is not a strategy course. It is not a "secret setup", not a new indicator
package, not a course, and not material claiming that one entry rule solves the
problem of trading.

This is not a managed service. It is not run for you, your broker is not
connected for you, your broker account is not managed for you, and your
market-data subscription, deployment, compliance decision, or live trading
decision is not taken over for you.

This is not an "install and make money" product. The correct order is
understanding first, then LEARNING / DEMO, tests, audit review, documentation,
and only after that any thinking toward broker-connected PAPER or LIVE.

This is not an AI trading operator either. Around QDE, the AI agent is for
review, checking, consistency analysis, and evidence-based work. It must not
turn off risk control, handle credentials, decide real-capital use, or override
an audit or fail-closed boundary.

If you are looking for a quick promise, a signal, or a ready-made live bot, this
is probably not the right direction for you.

If, however, you want to understand and use a system where the decision, risk,
execution, and audit path do not disappear into a black box, then you are in the
right place.
